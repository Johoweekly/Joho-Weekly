💸 Joho Payment Policy

At Joho, we’re mostly free — like the Wi-Fi at your local library. But if we ever launch paid stuff (exclusive zines, premium chaos, etc.), we’ll:

  • Be crystal clear about pricing.
  • Never sneak in hidden fees.
  • Accept payments via safe gateways.
  • Offer refunds if we mess up — not if you suddenly regret your life choices.

Payment comes before the newsletter. If there’s a delay in payment, we can’t send it. (Like, come on — we charge so little 😤)
